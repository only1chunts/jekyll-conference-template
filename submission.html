Present your work

While the main program of presentations is complete we are delighted to encourage as many participants as possible to create a poster presentation of their work to facilitate networking and sharing of ideas

All posters will be displayed within the venue for the duration of the meeting, with specific times set aside for presentation of the posters each day.

Poster Submission Instructions:

Submitted poster must be written in English, must be no larger than A0, and must include the author names and affiliations.

To submit your poster abstract, please follow below link to the submission site:

Poster Submission Site

Note that at least one of the authors of each poster must be registered to attend the meeting.
