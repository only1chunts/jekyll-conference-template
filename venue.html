The Genomics Standards Consortiums' 22nd workshop is scheduled for Nov 10-12, 2021 and will be held in Moorea, French Polynesia.

Conference Location:

Gump Research Station on the Island of Moorea, French Polynesia

The Gump Station regularly hosts classes and conferences. The Research Building is the primary venue for meetings; the Meeting room is climate controlled and can hold 30 to 40 in lecture-style layout. GSC-Moorea expects to receive 40 attendees including sponsors, speakers, local scientists, and GSC board members, many of which are directors and world leaders in their respective technology area.

Accomodations:

The station has several bungalows including hillside, waterfront as well as dormitory style capable of housing up to 15 people in shared accommodations. World class hotels are a short distance away for those who wish to stay off campus, choose from overwater bungalows or standard hotel rooms.
